APPLICATION PROCESS
Application period for ELTE Doctoral School of Informatics is from 1 April 2023 to 31 May 2023

Tuition fee for non-EEA citizens: 4500 EUR/semester.

Applicants to the Doctoral School of Informatics must have an MA, MBA or MSc degree, or shall be last year students at one of these programs.

Application platform:
https://doktorifelveteli.elte.hu/

LIST OF DOCUMENTS you need to upload for applying*:
*The documents are only accepted in the following languages: English or Hungarian. Official translations must be attached if the original language is different.

-copy of master’s degree certificate (last year students can bring it after the entrance examination)
-copy of BSc degree certificate
-transcript of records (both BSc and MSc)
-B2 complex language exam certificate (state-accredited)
-curriculum vitae
-list of publications (if you have)
-character reference/ recommendation letter
-research plan signed by the supervisor (list of thesis topics and supervisors: http://www.doktori.hu/index.php?menuid=116&lang=EN)
-medical certificate
-a statement about the payment of the tuition fee, if the applicant doesn’t apply for scholarship
-a statement about the place of the doctoral research work in case the applicant will do the research work at an external institute outside of the university
-certificate of clean criminal records
-proof of admission fee payment (9000 HUF)
