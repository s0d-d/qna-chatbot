ENROLMENT
After the successful admission, PhD students are required to enrol to the Doctoral School, in order to establish their student status.
The enrolment period is usually on the last week of August.

Documents to be submitted to room 2.317:
-ID card or passport
- residence permit
-1 passport photo
-original MSc diploma
-original language certificate
-study plan for 8 semesters (signed by supervisor)
-adult training contract
-enrolment sheet printed from Neptun
-statement about tuition fee
-signed scholarship agreement
