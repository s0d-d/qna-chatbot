The Faculty of Informatics of Eötvös Loránd University offers a wide range of bachelor programmes to international students who intend to obtain a bachelor’s degree at ELTE and who want to complete the entire program in English at ELTE in Budapest, Hungary. We recommend our courses especially to those who wish to enhance their knowledge in the field of informatics sciences. The bachelor programmes are accredited by the Hungarian Accreditation Committee. Diplomas issued at ELTE are acknowledged worldwide.
